PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: July 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to West Select ("we", "us", "our"). We provide a local e-commerce marketplace app (“Service”) where users can list, buy, and sell items. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you use our Android app built with Flutter, using Google Sign-In and Firebase backend services.

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. We comply with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and relevant Philippine laws.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Sign-In data: Your Google account ID, name, email address, profile photo (if shared).
  • Profile info you add: phone number, delivery/pickup address, product listings, chat messages.
  • Transaction data: items listed, items bought/sold, timestamps, ratings.
  • Usage & device data: IP address, device type, OS version, Firebase analytics data.
  • Cookies and similar tech: used by Firebase for secure sessions and app analytics.

We do not ask for sensitive personal data like government ID numbers, bank account passwords, or biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Data

We may use your data to:

  • Authenticate you (via Google Sign-In)
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app and features
  • Facilitate listings, chat, transactions, and reviews
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send important notifications (policy changes, account alerts)
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse
  • Analyze usage to improve performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Where & How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers, which may be located outside the Philippines (e.g., in Singapore, the US, or other Google data centers). By using the app, you consent to this transfer and processing outside your country.

We rely on Google’s security measures, encryption, and access controls to protect your data.

5. Sharing of Data

We may share your data:

  • With Firebase & Google services (for sign-in, hosting, analytics)
  • With local third-party service providers (e.g., IT contractors, analytics)
  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of West Select, our users, or the public

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Your Rights

Under Philippine law, you have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated data
  • Request deletion (where legally allowed)
  • Withdraw consent or object to certain uses
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)

Contact us at cs.westselect@gmail.com to make these requests. We may need to verify your identity.

7. Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active and for as long as necessary for our legal obligations and dispute resolution. Chat history and transaction logs may be kept to prevent fraud and support future claims.

8. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion anytime by emailing cs.westselect@gmail.com. Note: deleting your account removes your profile and listings but may not remove data needed to comply with the law (e.g., transaction logs).

9. Children

The app is intended only for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Security

We apply industry-standard encryption, secure access controls, and regular reviews to protect your data. Still, no method is 100% secure; use the app responsibly.

11. Updates

We may revise this policy; major changes will be notified in-app or via email. Continued use means you accept the changes.
